About CODM

Call of Duty: Mobile is a first-person multiplayer battle game which can be played on iOS, Android devices as well as using a web browser. It is the latest mobile version from the Call of Duty franchise, which has a range of other Call of Duty games available on popular games consoles.

Manta Ray

Naomi Mizushima (Japanese: 水島直美) also known as Manta Ray is a character featured in Call of Duty Online and Call of Duty: Mobile.

Ghost

Special Operations forces, a mysterious group known only as "Ghosts", lead the battle against a newly-emerged, technologically-superior, global power.

PRICE

Captain John Price is a playable operator, specifically the "Skirmisher" skin, which players can obtain by pre-ordering Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III and linking their Activision account.

Serine

Mythic characters are premium-tier skins that offer a higher level of customisation and visual effects compared to legendary and epic skins.

The Mythic Kilo 141 "Demonsong" is a popular weapon skin in Call of Duty Mobile (CODM), and it's been known to return in the game's draws periodically.

The Mythic AK-47 "Radiance" in Call of Duty Mobile (CODM) is a premium-tier skin that was part of the Ultraviolet Mythic Draw, offering unique animations and visual effects, and has returned in a recent update.

The mythic FFAR-1 is called "Bright Blade" and is known for its unique sword animation and visually striking design, making it a popular and sought-after weapon skin.

TOURNAMENT

"Tournament Mode" is a competitive in-game mode where players can earn COD Points and other rewards by participating in weekly tournaments, running from Friday to Sunday
